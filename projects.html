Seaperch

Seaperch Robot

Summer camp that took place during highschool where I was tasked with creating a Seaperch robot, then modifying that robot to perform various tasks in a course. A Seaperch is a simple water based robot made mostly of PVC and some simple electronics. This was a very enlightening project as it was the first time my real world problem solving skills were really tested. It was an interesting learning experience having to iterate the various tools used on the Seaperch over and over until one design was found that worked. It was also interesting as we had to make the controller from scrath (soldering the circuit board together) and build the Seaperch itself.
