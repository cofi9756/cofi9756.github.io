A little more about me

I think the thing that excites me the most about the field of computer science is the possibilities that computer science offers, both career wise and creativity wise. Most fields nowadays use computers for some task or another, meaning with a computer science degree, you can get some kind of job in really any industry as long as you have the other skills, which can be easily obtained with a minor or another degree. On the creative side, I think it is incredible what can be done with modern computers that people only 50 years ago would have thought impossible, from advanced video games with complex systems and graphics to highly advanced scientific simulations catapulting science further than ever, I think both are very cool and I think it is even cooler that both can be done with the same technology behind them. I think the main thing that concerns me as someone entering the field is the main thing that concerns anyone entering the field right now, the general state of the computer science job market, as more jobs are lost to AI or companies simply stringing candidates along or posting ghost listings, it is getting harder and harder to get your foot in the door of the computer science industry. What I mainly hope to get from this course is connections, either making them or learning how to make them. As of right now, I currently struggle to make connections with people in the field, whether that be recruiters or professionals, which makes it difficult for me to be able to get jobs as I do not know anyone
